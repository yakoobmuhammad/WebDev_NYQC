The Nazeer Yakoob Quality and Compliance Inc (NYQC).

The NYQC is a Management System Consulting and Training Services company. Based in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. The organization provides Management System services to a broad range of organizations, both service and manufacturing.

  • Our principals have extensive experience in executive management, third party auditing and management system development.
  • Our philosophy is to form partnerships with our clients that will provide mutual benefit and success through full client engagement
  • Our belief is that management systems should be unique to a company and be structured to yield continual business improvement and improved customer satisfaction.
  • Our approach ensures that the corporate direction and objectives are understood and that the management system is designed to facilitate these goals
  • Our methods of service ensures the transfer of knowledge and management system ownership to our client.

Senior Consultant: Nazeer Mohammed

Nazeer Mohammed Bsc., President, Nazeer Yakoob Quality and Compliance Inc, has been in the Quality Management and Medical Device industry since 1995. Had been a QA /RA Manager for diverse industries including fortune 500 company Medical Device company in the North America.

Over twenty years combined experience in Quality Assurance, Regulatory Affairs and Quality Management System Auditing and Lean Six Sigma in Medical Device Industry: Registered Lead Auditor with a specialization in FDA, Canadian MDR, EU MDD/MDR, Jpal and CE marking. Particular strengths in ISO 9001, ISO 13485, MDSAP, CE, MDD/MDR, Auditing and Implementation and Regulatory Compliance, Third Party and Internal Compliance Audits such as ISO 9001, CMDCAS, MDSAP and MDD and Project Management.

Nazeer Mohammed is the President and Founder of NYQC. His Academic achievements include BSC in Industrial Management from KFUPM, Dhahran, KSA.
